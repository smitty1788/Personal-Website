About


I am a Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Specialist at Hood College in Frederick, MD. I currently teach ENSP103: Introduction to GIS and work on a variety of geospatial and remote sensing projects with the Center for Coastal and Watershed Studies.

Some of my interests are:

  • Hydrographic Surveying
  • Remote Sensing
  • Geodemographic Analysis
  • Terrestrial Laser Scanning
  • Geospatial Predictive Policing Stratiegies
  • Historic Mapping
